1159 We propose AXAF ACIS observations of the two brightest elliptical galaxies which have very low metal abundance determinations (~0.1 solar) for their X-ray emitting gas. Both galaxies have high quality optically determined stellar abundance profiles, indicating ~solar central stellar abundances. The high spatial and spectral resolution of AXAF ACIS observations will allow us to directly compare the metal abundance of the X-ray emitting gas to the stellar metallicity at a range of radii within the optical half light radii. This is a companion proposal to one in which the target galaxies have a much smaller disparity in their measured stellar and gaseous abundances.
