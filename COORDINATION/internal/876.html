2619 We propose to measure the X-ray flux of 27 previously undetected, bright, radio quiet quasars with $z>4$. To date, only 6 radio quiet quasars with $z>4$ have been detected in X-rays. We draw our sample from the objects whose emission line spectra have been observed by Shields and Hamman with Keck and the MMT. These well-studied objects form the definitive sample of the highest redshift quasars known.
