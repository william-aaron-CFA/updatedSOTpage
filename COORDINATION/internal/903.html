1366 We propose to observe three well-studied cluster gravitational lenses, which are amongst the most X-ray luminous and massive clusters of galaxies known at intermediate redshifts, to determine the (mass-weighted) temperature profiles and total mass profiles in these systems. The observations will permit direct, accurate measurements of the X-ray gas, total mass and metallicity distributions in the clusters, from their core regions (on the scale of tens of kpc) out to the limits of X-ray detection (r ~3Mpc). The data will reveal the impact of cooling flows on X-ray mass measurements for clusters and elucidate the origin of the discrepancies between X-ray and strong lensing masses for systems without cooling flows.
