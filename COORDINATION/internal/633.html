2140 We propose to use ACIS-I to image the centers of the starburst analogues NGC 3603 in the Galaxy and NGC 346 in the SMC. This study will enable us to definitively determine the effects of abundance on X-ray emission, will provide X-ray luminosities for nearly 100 stars, will provide X-ray spectra for dozens of massive stars, and will enable us to examine the nature of diffuse emission and SNR on the local ISM.
