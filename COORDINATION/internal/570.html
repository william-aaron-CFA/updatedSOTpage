3470 Abell 1060 is an extended, nearly isothermal (kT=4 keV) source of X-rays. The X-ray spectrum of A1060 is dominated my strong emission lines from Fe, Ne, Mg, Si, S, Ca, and Ar. This source will be observed periodically by all 4 focal plane instruments throughout the AXAF mission to monitor any temperoal variations in the characteristics of the detectors. This observation is the first such measurement and needs to be carried out shortly after launch to establish a fiducial point from which subsequent observations will be compared. A1060 is well suited to monitor changes in the CTI of the CCDs due to radiation damage, since it is a good source of lines in addition to those avaiable with the internal ACIS calibration source.
