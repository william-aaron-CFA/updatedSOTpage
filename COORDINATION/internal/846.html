1871 If FR~I radio galaxies and BL~Lac objects are parts of a single population, and only their orientation causes them to appear different (the BL~Lac ``unification'' scheme), then they should have the same isotropic properties. We recently extended existing radio and optical tests to the X-ray by searching near BL~Lacs for X-ray emission which matches the X-ray envelopes of FR~I objects found by Worrall \& Birkinshaw (1994). The well-known object PKS~0521-365 shows an unexpectedly strong halo when observed with the ROSAT HRI. Here we propose to use ACIS-I to obtain images and spectroscopy for PKS~0521-365 and three other BL Lacs. The data will determine the physical relationship between halos and their AGN and test the unification scheme.
