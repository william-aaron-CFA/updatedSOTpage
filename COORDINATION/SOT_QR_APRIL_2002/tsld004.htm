SOT MP Ongoing Activities

    Scott Wolk

    SAO

    23-Apr-02

  • Weekly Scheduling

    • Supplied regular lists of ORs to FOT MP team for scheduling.
      • Worked with instrument specialists and observers (through USINT) to update plans and modify ORs, as necessary.
    • Supported load reviews.
      • Distributed planning products to SI and CXC groups for review.
      • Reviewed Mission Schedule; participated in formal review telecons.
      • Documented items reviewed in SOT MP load reviews.
    • Since Jan 15, 2002, roughly 230 observations have been scheduled for a total observing time of 4.8 million seconds (including Calibration targets).
      • Missed observations due to radiation events not reported here; see FOT/MP-MTA report.
      • Scheduled efficiency: 66.3% (obstime/total), 85.3% (obstime/total-radzone-CTI).
    • Maintained long-term schedule, including re-observation of incomplete programs and new calibrations.

  • TOO/DDT Support

    • Executed TOO/DDT programs and new calibrations.

  • Multi-telescope Observations

    • Since Jan 15, 2002, coordinated 4 observing programs with other observatories (including HST, RXTE, and SAX).
    • Considerable effort also expended on rescheduling of coordinated programs affected by HST servicing mission and FUSE operations anomalies.
