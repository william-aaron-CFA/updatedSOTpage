    ACA Operations and Ground Aspect Solution


  • All hardware subsystems required for post-facto celestial location are functioning well:

    • ACA is tracking stars down to 10.9 mag.
    • Occasional loss of fid light due to strong ionizing radiation events striking readout window background pixels.
    • FLCA and fid lights are functioning per spec.
    • FTS (periscope) is stable (though see below).

  • FOT is performing final testing of Star Viewer, which is a Matlab tool to perform ACA star selection and serve as the prototype/testbed for new SSA requirements.

  • Several improvements to ACA load review tools, in particular the use of high-fidelity estimates of maneuver errors for checking star catalogs.
