    SAO

    23-Apr-02

  • In support of FOT studies of the effect of elevated temperatures on EPHIN, we have examined the EPHIN coincidence event PHA records for evidence of detector degradation.

    • No change is apparent in the width of the proton peak in the mass-spectrum.
    • A slight change in the mean of the proton peak is observed, which may indicate a change in performance cause by elevated temperatures or the radiation exposure.
    • Consulting with the EPHIN IPI team on analysis results.

  • Supporting FOT study of EPHIN failure flag/timing anomaly.
