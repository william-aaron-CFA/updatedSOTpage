ACIS Threshold Plane Latch-up

    Scott Wolk

    SAO

    23-Apr-02

    The latch-up situation was discussed at some length by the ACIS Science operations group. They have proceeded along 2 parallel courses of action:


    • Power-cycle the FEPs before every run that computes fresh bias maps.
    • Figure out why the BEP trickles bias maps; develop a S/W patch.

    Status:

    #1 is now fully implemented: the FEPs are recycled before each run in which a bias map is updated. This ensures that a T-plane latchup will only affect a limited number of science runs. Since we have implemented this, we have not observed a single T-plane latchup.
